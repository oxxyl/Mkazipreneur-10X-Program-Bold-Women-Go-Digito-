Dataset 03 · Expression of Interest

Expression of Interest (EOI)

874 unique MSMEs completed the EOI assessment to participate in the next phase (high-touch support) of the program. By design, applicants could also submit details on behalf of their employers or colleagues — producing 1,161 individual applicant records in total. The pool is overwhelmingly young women solopreneurs, with inclusion targets for PWDs and refugees met at the application stage.
